This tone indicator is used to indicate you're asking a {frontmatter.fullForm}. It is occasionally used as **{frontmatter.aliases}**.

<br />
<br />

## Why should I use this?

Some people use this tone indicator exclusively for questions when using **/gen** for statements.

<br />

## Examples within messages:

- Are you sure about that? {frontmatter.tag}
- Do you need me to explain? {frontmatter.tag}
- Clarify? {frontmatter.tag}
